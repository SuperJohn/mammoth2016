Schedule of Events
Thursday - Sunday, March 18th-20th, 2016

Thursday: Arrive in Mammoth Lakes

Afternoon: We recommend that guests arrive in Mammoth on Thursday night, so you can acclimate to the elevation and participate in Friday’s fun events! The town of Mammoth Lakes is approximately 8,000 feet above sea level. For those of you who don’t know what this means – head over to our FAQ section. You’ll thank us later.


5:00 pm: Welcome Cocktail Hour: For anyone who does arrive early to town, Christine Houghton will be hosting a St. Patrick’s Day cocktail hour at the Westin. Sarah will soon be changing her Irish last name, so let’s raise our glasses to Sarah’s last few days as a Sullivan!


6:00 pm: Shuttle from San Francisco: For guests leaving from the San Francisco area, we may have a shuttle to help you brave the winding roads. For more information, see the shuttle section of the Travel and Lodging page. Please make sure to let us know if you think you may want to hitch a ride.

Friday: Snow Day & Welcome Dinner

All Day: Snow Day!!!

9:00am: Meetup at Main Lodge: Let’s hit the slopes! John and Sarah love snowboarding, and they want to share California’s best snow with you. We’ll have a guided route with stops along the way that are suitable for both beginners and advanced skiers. Please RSVP when you get your official invitation, so we can look into discounted tickets.


10:00am: Tube Park Mania: Don’t snowboard or ski? A group will be at the tube park making snow angels and enjoying this winter wonderland.


12:00pm: Lunch on Mammoth Mountain at Canyon Lodge: Whether you’re snowboarding or not, we would love to see you for a mid-day bite to eat. Canyon Lodge is accessible via the free gondola from the Village.


Friday Evening: Welcome Dinner

6:00pm: Welcome Dinner: Bring your Steins and break out your Lederhosen & Dirndls – Everyone is invited to a German Style reception at Mammoth Brewing Company! Guests are encouraged to dress in traditional garb, as we make merry at this quintessential Mammoth beer hall.

Saturday: Our Wonderous Winter Wedding

Morning & Afternoon: Have a Great Day in Mammoth: While the wedding party is preparing for the big event, we hope you’ll enjoy some of Mammoth’s Best. Sarah and John’s Favorites Include

  • Hot Springs
  • Cross Country Skiing or Snowshoeing at Tamarack
  • Take a Scenic Gondola Ride to the top of Mammoth Mountain. The views are spectacular.
  • Bowling and Indoor Golfing at Mammoth Activity Center
  • Workout at Snow Creek Athletic Center

4:45 pm:: Shuttle from the Village to Main Lodge.


5:00 pm: The Gondola Opens! Guests will be transported to our mid-mountain chalet by gondola from Main Lodge.


5:30 pm: The Ceremony Begins: Weather permitting, Sarah and John will tie the knot outside on the sun deck at McCoy station. Please bring a jacket to stay warm.


Dress Code: Black Tie Invited. Please be conscious of weather, and stay warm!


6:00 pm: Cocktail Hour: Move inside to Parallax, and let the celebrations begin!


7:00 pm: Reception: Sarah and John have quite the party planned for you. They’re excited to welcome one of their favorite bands – The California Honeydrops! Get in the grove, and check out their tunes before you get to the mountain.


11:30 pm: After Party at Parallax: You don’t have to go down the mountain yet! The DJ will be playing late, and we’ll dance the night away at 9,000ft.

Sunday: Leave with Love

Sunday: Brunch at the Sullivan’s House

10:00am – 12:00pm: Katie and Dan will host a farewell brunch at their home in Mammoth. Come hungry and leave the newlyweds with a hug on your way out of town.
