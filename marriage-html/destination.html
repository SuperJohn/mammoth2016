Flying to Mammoth Airport Code: MMH

Guests can fly nonstop to Mammoth Lakes (MMH) from Los Angeles, San Francisco San Diego, and Denver.


From Mammoth Yosemite Airport (MMH), you are only a 10 minute drive to Mammoth Lakes. Please see visitmammoth.com or check with your hotel for information about Airport Shuttles available to you.


If you do decide to fly, we recommend that you leave ample time to arrive before the wedding and that you arrange a contingency plan for poor weather. Flights are often canceled in the winter, and we would hate for you to miss a powder day!
