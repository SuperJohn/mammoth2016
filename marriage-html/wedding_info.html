Schedule of Events
Thursday - Sunday, March 18th-20th, 2016

Thursday: Arrive in Mammoth Lakes

Evening: Arrive in Mammoth Lakes


We recommend that guests arrive in Mammoth on Thursday night, so you can acclimate to the elevation and participate in Friday’s fun events! The town of Mammoth Lakes is approximately 8,000 feet above sea level. For those of you who don’t know what this means – head over to our FAQ section. You’ll thank us later.


6 - 8pm: Welcome Cocktail Hour at the Westin Hotel


For anyone who does arrive early to town, Christine Houghton will be hosting a St. Patrick’s Day cocktail hour at the Westin Hotel. Sarah will soon be changing her Irish last name, so let’s raise our glasses to Sarah’s last few days as a Sullivan!


Libations and appetizers will be served from 6-8pm. Attire is casual - please come as you are! The event is located in the Foxfir banquet room at the Westin Hotel.

6:00 pm: Shuttle from San Francisco


For guests leaving from the San Francisco area, we may have a shuttle to help you brave the winding roads. For more information, see the shuttle section of the Travel and Lodging page. Please make sure to let us know if you think you may want to hitch a ride.

Friday: Snow Day & Octoberfest-themed Welcome Dinner

All Day: Snow Day!!!

9:00am: Boarders & Skiers Meetup at Main Lodge


Let’s hit the slopes! John and Sarah love snowboarding, and they want to share California’s best snow with you. We’ll have a guided route with stops along the way that are suitable for both beginners and advanced skiers. Please RSVP when you get your official invitation, so we can look into discounted tickets.


10:00am: Tube Park Mania

Don’t snowboard or ski? Have small kids? A group will be at the tube park sledding, making snow angels, and enjoying this winter wonderland. Learn more about Wooley's Tube Park here!


12:00pm: Lunch on Mammoth Mountain at Canyon Lodge


Whether you’re snowboarding or not, we would love to see you for a mid-day bite to eat. Canyon Lodge is accessible from The Village via a free Gondola and DOES NOT require a lift ticket. This is a very casual affair and is just a chance for people to grab a quick bite together and share some laughs.


6:00 - 9:00pm: Octoberfest-themed Welcome Dinner

Bring your Steins and break out your Lederhosen & Dirndls – Everyone is invited to a German Style reception at Mammoth Brewing Company! Guests are encouraged to dress in traditional garb, as we make merry at this quintessential Mammoth beer hall.


Attire: Many of us will be in full Lederhosen & Dirndls, but feel free to come as you please.

  • Walking distance from the Westin and The Village
  • Parking on site
  • Children Welcome

Saturday: Our Wonderous Winter Wedding

Morning & Afternoon:


Have a Great Day in Mammoth. While the wedding party is preparing for the big event, we hope you’ll enjoy some of Mammoth’s Best. Sarah and John’s Favorites Include

  • Thrillest Magazine's 11 Awesome Things to do in Mammoth
  • Hot Springs
  • Cross Country Skiing or Snowshoeing at Tamarack
  • Take a Scenic Gondola Ride to the top of Mammoth Mountain. The views are spectacular.
  • Bowling and Indoor Golfing at Mammoth Activity Center
  • Workout at Snow Creek Athletic Center

4:30 pm: Board Shuttle @ The Village


We request that guests take the bus up to the Main Lodge. Busses depart from the Westin at 4:10, 4:40, and 4:45. A bus will depart from the Shiloh Inn at 4pm. Ater arriving at the Main Lodge, guests will board the mountain gondola to McCoy Station, site of the wedding at 9,000 feet. Guests who board the 4:00 or 4:10 bus will have the opportunity to go to Mammoth Mountain Summit (11k') prior to the wedding (recommended). Busses will depart back down the hill every half hour beginning at 9:30pm and ending at 1am.


4:45 pm: The Gondola Opens!


Guests will be transported to our mid-mountain chalet by gondola from Main Lodge.


5:30 pm: The Ceremony Begins Weather permitting, Sarah and John will tie the knot outside on the sun deck at McCoy station. Please bring a jacket to stay warm.



6:00 pm: Cocktail Hour @ Parallax with Warren Priske's Jazz Band

7:00 pm: Dinner at McCoy Station

9 pm - 1 am: Late Night - Party at Parallax

Sarah and John have quite the party planned for you. We'll have a full bar and the party at McCoy Station will be going until 1am. When guests are ready to leave, we have a shuttle running every 15 mins from Main Lodge to the Village.

Sunday: Leave with Love

Sunday: Brunch at the Sullivan’s House

9:30am – 12:30pm: Katie and Dan will host a farewell brunch at their home in Mammoth. Come hungry and leave the newlyweds with a hug before they depart on their honeymoon.
