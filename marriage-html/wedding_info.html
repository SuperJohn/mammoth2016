Schedule of Events
Friday - Sunday, March 18th-20th, 2016

Thursday: Arrive in Mammoth Lakes

We recommend that guests arrive Thursday evening or early Friday in order to enjoy all that Mammoth has to offer, spend time with us and together, and join in on some events.

Friday Day: Have Fun!!!

Our grand ambition on Friday is to get as many of our skiing / snowboarding friends and family out on the mountain as is possible. It would be so much fun to see you all out there. While we won't all be able to ride together in a large group of 50 people, we'll all be out there, in our own groups of friends, family members, and new acquaintances having a great time. We'll meet up for a big group lunch at McCoy Station on the Mountain. McCoy Station is accessible to non-skiiers as well and is the best way to take in the scenery that Mammoth Mountain has to offer, so non-skiiers are also encouraged to meet up for a lunch around noon.

Friday Evening: Rehearsal Dinner
